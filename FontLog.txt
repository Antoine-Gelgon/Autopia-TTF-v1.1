FONTLOG for Autopia
-------------------
This file provides detailed information on the Autopia font family software.
This information should be distributed along with the Autopia font family fonts
and any derivative works.

Basic Font Information
--------------------------
Copyright (c) 2014, Antoine Gelgon (http://antoine-gelgon.fr/)

Autopia is a free family Typeface designed by Antoine Gelgon. This project was initiated during the Summer School of OSP in August 2013. The particularity of this fonts is in the process of developing its shape. The skeleton was generated through Autotrace Program (a program that converts bitmap to vectorgraphics).
